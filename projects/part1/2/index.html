Working Dogs

Herding Dogs | Service Dogs

Working dogs are trained to perform important tasks such as guiding, herding, and rescue.

  • Guide visually impaired people
  • Herd livestock
  • Search and rescue

Working dogs are specially trained animals that perform important jobs alongside humans. They guide people who are visually impaired, herd livestock on farms, search for survivors in disasters, and even work with police and military units. Unlike pets, their daily lives are built around focus, discipline, and teamwork, showing how deeply connected humans and dogs can be.
